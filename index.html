Crystal Collector!

At the start of the game, you will be given a random target number.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each number is hidden to you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change!
